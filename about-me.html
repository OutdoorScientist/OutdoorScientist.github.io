About me

1. Work Ethic and Attitude Motivation

Motivation:

I’m driven by the opportunity to take on challenging yet meaningful work, especially when it allows room for creativity and problem-solving. I find fulfillment in roles that push me to grow, make an impact, and think outside the box. When I’m in a position that combines these elements, I feel fortunate and deeply motivated to contribute my best.

Work style:

I am highly capable of working independently and taking ownership of my tasks, which allows me to stay focused and deliver results efficiently. However, I also thrive in a team environment, as I enjoy collaborating, exchanging ideas, and contributing to shared goals. I find that balancing independence with teamwork helps me perform at my best and adapt to different situations.

Resilience:

At AVEVA, I worked on a three-month project that, despite being completed and merged into the main branch, was reverted shortly after because the stakeholders were dissatisfied with the workflow—ironically, the one they had requested. This experience taught me the importance of maintaining clear communication and setting expectations with stakeholders throughout a project. While the outcome was disappointing, I used it as an opportunity to reflect, remain composed, and adapt my approach. It strengthened my ability to handle setbacks with professionalism and focus on learning and improving for future projects.

2. Cultural Fit

Values alignment:

I place a high value on delivering quality, ethical, and meaningful work. Integrity and purpose are central to my approach, and I strive to make a positive impact through my contributions. My mission is to work with companies that have a clear sense of purpose and align with my commitment to ethical practices and delivering lasting value.

Interpersonal skills:

I collaborate effectively with a diverse range of individuals and teams. At AVEVA, I worked with colleagues across the United States and internationally, navigating time zones and cultural differences to achieve shared goals. At Terracon, I coordinated closely with drillers and clients in the field, while also keeping project managers across the country informed to ensure smooth project execution. My experience has taught me to adapt my communication style, listen actively, and build strong working relationships, no matter the team or setting.

Adaptability:

During my time at Terracon as a field geologist, I honed my ability to thrive in loud, fast-paced, and dynamically changing environments and contracted drilling teams. This experience taught me how to quickly adapt to new workflows, personalities, and challenges. As a result, I’ve become highly adaptable and find it natural to adjust to varying company cultures and dynamics while maintaining productivity and building strong working relationships.

3. Interests and Hobbies

Creativity:

I have a variety of completed and ongoing programming projects that demonstrate my creativity and problem-solving abilities, each incorporating different technologies, skill sets, and knowledge areas. For example, my heavy-duty robotic arm project combines object detection, Python programming, and computer interfacing with an understanding of physics, design principles, and engineering. This project reflects my ability to integrate diverse disciplines to solve complex challenges while showcasing both technical expertise and creative thinking.

Team activities:

I am an avid rock climber, particularly in sport climbing, which requires a high degree of trust, communication, and teamwork to ensure safety and success. Beyond climbing, I’ve also demonstrated teamwork and leadership in professional settings. For instance, during my internship, I took on the role of scrum master, effectively coordinating the team to complete the project on time. Additionally, I led drilling teams on-site at Terracon, overseeing over 75 fault-free drilling operations. These experiences reflect my ability to lead, collaborate, and achieve goals in both recreational and professional environments.

Well-roundedness:

I’m passionate about my programming projects, but I also enjoy a variety of hands-on and creative activities outside of work. I work on vehicles, which sharpens my problem-solving and technical skills. I play music, which allows me to be creative through improvisation. I paint, which helps me relax and think visually. Additionally, I enjoy training my dogs, which has taught me patience and the value of consistent effort. These hobbies provide a well-rounded balance to my life and reflect my diverse interests and skills.
