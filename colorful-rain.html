Colorful Rain

Discover a simple and soothing way to relax. OutdoorScientists Rain Pop invites you into a calming world where colorful droplets fall gently from the top of your screen. Tap to pop them, enjoy soft animations, and let your mind unwind. Whether you’re taking a short break, easing stress, or just want something peaceful to focus on, this minimalist game helps you reset with quiet clarity. Switch effortlessly between Day Mode and Night Mode to match your mood — bright and airy during the day, or soft and tranquil at night. Features include:

  • Pop colorful water droplets as they fall
  • Enjoy smooth, relaxing animations
  • Switch between Day and Night themes
  • Calming visual design for stress relief
  • No timers, no pressure — just tap and relax
  • Perfect for quick breaks or mindfulness moments
  • Relax your mind, focus your senses, and enjoy a peaceful escape — one droplet at a time.

Troubleshooting

First and foremost, try restarting the app to see if that resolves the issue. If problems persist, consider reinstalling the app to ensure you have the latest version. For further assistance, feel free to reach out to me via email or connect on LinkedIn.
